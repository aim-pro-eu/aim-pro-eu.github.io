**Welcome to the debut issue of the AIM-PRO newsletter!** 

AIM-PRO (AI Literacy for Multidisciplinary Professional Readiness and Outreach) is officially underway, bringing together 15 partners across 9 European countries to advance AI literacy and innovation. This issue highlights our kick-off meeting in L’Aquila, where we mapped stakeholder needs, explored AI literacy frameworks, and launched collaborative initiatives to enhance AI literacy for education and professional training. 

Discover our vision, goals and upcoming events, and learn how you can get involved in fostering AI literacy across Europe.
