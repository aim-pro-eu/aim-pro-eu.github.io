The **AIM-PRO – AI Literacy for Multidisciplinary Professional Readiness and Outreach** project is excited to announce the launch of its official [**LinkedIn page** ](https://www.linkedin.com/company/aim-pro-eu)and **[YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@AIM-PRO-EU)**. These platforms will serve as additional hubs for sharing project updates, event highlights and valuable resources, fostering engagement with stakeholders, educators and the wider community.

The YouTube channel debuts with a video, produced by Università degli Studi dell’Aquila, capturing the AIM-PRO Kick-off Meeting held in L’Aquila on 10-11 February 10-11, 2026. It introduces the project’s goals, key objectives, and the initial steps of its implementation.

**Watch the kick-off video [here](https://youtu.be/ubFHipHqp_0?si=kCvJCgGZEATC1TnD).**

**Follow the AIM-PRO on LinkedIn and subscribe to the YouTube channel** to stay updated on upcoming webinars, project activities, and our latest developments in AI literacy and professional readiness.
