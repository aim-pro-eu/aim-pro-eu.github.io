## 2. Reasons for Collecting Your Data

We collect and process your data to support the implementation and objectives of the **AIM-PRO** Erasmus+ Project, in line with its defined purpose and work plan..

* **Project Services and Communication:** To provide you with information related to the **AIM-PRO** Erasmus+ project, including updates on project activities, results, opportunities, and events.
* **Legitimate Interest:** We only use your data in ways you would reasonably expect, proportionate to our legitimate interests, balancing them against your individual rights and freedoms.

- - -

## 3. What Data Do We Collect?

We strive to collect as little personal data as we need to further our intended purpose and activities. We collect:

* **Identifying information:** Surname, first name, function, organization or institution, e-mail address, and postal address.
* **Communications:** By email or other correspondence.

- - -

## 4. How Do We Collect Your Data?

* **Direct Interaction:** Most of your data is communicated to us by your active intervention (e.g., when you subscribe to the AIM-PRO quarterly newsletter).
* **Third Parties:** When we obtain personal data from third parties, we always process and treat your data in accordance with this Policy and the current EU Regulation.

- - -

## 5. How Do We Store Your Data?

* **Access:** The **AIM-PRO** Consortium maintains databases containing participant information. Access to these databases is restricted to authorized staff members of the consortium and project partners involved in the implementation of the project, and the data is used exclusively for purposes related to the project’s activities.
* **Retention:** We keep your data only for the time necessary to accomplish the “Intended purpose and activities” of the **AIM-PRO** Erasmus+ Project.
* **Security:** All members of **AIM-PRO** Consortium are made aware of security procedures. **AIM-PRO** Data Controller ensures appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect against loss, unauthorized access, destruction, misuse, or improper disclosure.

- - -

## 6. Data Sharing

Your personal data is not shared outside the AIM-PRO Consortium and associated partners, except where required for reporting and verification purposes to funding bodies. Where such reporting is required, data may be shared only within Europe, including Switzerland and the United Kingdom. Appropriate safeguards are in place to ensure that your data is used solely for the agreed project-related purposes. 

- - -

## 7. Your Rights and How to Exercise Them

You have the following rights regarding your data:

1. **Right to be informed** about the collection and use of your personal data.
2. **Right of access** to your personal data.
3. **Right to rectification** of inaccurate personal data.
4. **Right to erasure** (the “right to be forgotten”).
5. **Right to request restriction** or suppression of your personal data.
6. **Right to data portability** (obtain and reuse your data).
7. **Right to object** to data processing.

> **To exercise these rights:** Contact us at **rpd@strutture.univaq.it**. We respond without undue delay, within the time provided by the current EU GDPR.

## 8. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies Policy

### Definitions and Standards

Cookies are small text files that may be sent to and registered on your computer by the websites you visit to "remember" your actions and preferences (login, language, etc.).

### Types of Cookies Used

**AIM-PRO** only uses technical cookies:

* **Browsing or session cookies:** Strictly necessary for the website’s operation. Automatically deleted when you close your browser.

### Browser Settings

You can block or delete cookies used on the **AIM-PRO** website via your browser options:

* [Internet Explorer](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/delete-and-manage-cookies-168dab11-0753-043d-7c16-ede5947fc64d)
* [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enhanced-tracking-protection-firefox-desktop)
* [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
* [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac)

**CAUTION:** If you block technical cookies, the website may become impossible to browse or certain functions may become unavailable.
